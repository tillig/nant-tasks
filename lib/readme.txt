To reduce download size, I did not include the NAnt assemblies needed to compile in this folder.

Get a copy of NAnt from 0.86b1 from http://nant.sourceforge.net/ and put in this folder:
* NAnt.Core.dll
* NAnt.DotNetTasks.dll
* NAnt.NUnit.dll
* NAnt.NUnit2Tasks.dll

Or, optionally, change the references in the project to point to your locally installed version of NAnt.
